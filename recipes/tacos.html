Description

A zesty and fresh taco recipe sure to please the pickiest of humans that eat.

Ingredients

  • 8 Fresh Corn Tortillas
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1 cup guacamole
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 pound ground beef or alternative meat
  • 1 package taco seasoning
  • 1 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup minced jalapeno pepper
  • 1 lime (cut into wedges)

Steps

  1. Place tortillas in glass dish in oven on 150F and let warm while preparing rest of ingredients.
  2. Cook beef and taco seasoning package together in pan on low heat until mostly brown.
  3. Once meat is finished, pull tortillas out of oven and cover in all your favorite toppings.
