Pan Fried PB & J

back Home

Description

An addictive spin on your favorite classic.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil or oil of choice
  • 4 slices bread
  • 2 tablespoons fruit preserve
  • 2 tablespoons of favorite nut butter
  • cinnamon powder
  • turmeric powder

Steps

  1. Add oil and 4 slices of bread into pan and heat until just starting to turn brown on both sides.
  2. Turn off heat and put 1 tablespoon of preserve and butter each per pair of bread slices.
  3. Allow preserves and butter to warm before removing slices from pan and assembling into sandwiches.
  4. Garnish with cinnamon and turmeric.
